Don't Over Think Shit

This print was inspired by a neon sign owned by Kenny Beats. I made it by carving out a piece of wood, inking the wood, and then running it through a printing press. I utilized a technique called a rainbow roll to get a gradient in this piece.

Ego Death

This piece was inspired by the concept of ego death. This piece combined two printmaking methods -- monoprinting and relief printing. I utilized a technique called a rainbow roll to achieve the gradient.

Hair Pick

These hair picks are made out of walnut, have a powdered silver inlay, and are finished with a natural beeswax. I cut the picks out of a solid piece of wood using a scroll saw. I sanded the piece up to 4000 grit, adding a taper along the way. The engraving for the names was done using the laser cutter and was inlayed with powdered silver and CA glue. I went with a beeswax and orange oil finish in order to make the piece safe for use on hair.

Bookshelf

I made this simple bookshelf over the course of three days during my winter break in 2020-2021. I utilized lumber from a home improvement store. I cut everything down to the appropriate size and sanded to 220 grit. Then I stained the wood and assembled it using shelf brackets. This piece was done entirely using hand tools.
